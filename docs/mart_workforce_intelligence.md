# Workforce Intelligence Mart

## Objective

The Workforce Intelligence Mart serves as the centralized analytical layer of the platform.

It consolidates workforce, performance, wellbeing, attendance and retention information into a single business-ready dataset that supports:

- Executive dashboards
- Workforce analytics
- Attrition prediction
- Burnout detection
- Talent development analysis
- Explainable AI models
- GenAI Workforce Advisor

## Business Domains

### Employee

Core employee information.

### Performance

Employee productivity and contribution metrics.

### Wellbeing

Indicators related to employee wellbeing and workload.

### Attendance

Absenteeism and work attendance indicators.

### Retention

Attrition and turnover-related indicators.

### Talent Development

Training, promotions and career progression indicators.

## Analytical Scores

### Burnout Risk Score

Measures potential employee burnout risk.

### Attrition Risk Score

Measures employee turnover risk.

### Promotion Readiness Score

Measures promotion potential.

### Workforce Load Index

Measures workload intensity.

### Workforce Efficiency Index

Measures productivity relative to workload.

### Growth Index

Measures employee development and career progression.

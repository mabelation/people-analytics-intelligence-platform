# Project Charter

## Project Name:

### People Analytics Intelligence Platform with GenAI Workforce Advisor

---

## Business Problem

Human Resources data is frequently distributed across multiple systems such as payroll, attendance, performance management, and employee surveys.

This fragmentation limits visibility into workforce trends and makes strategic decision-making difficult.

Organizations need a centralized platform capable of integrating workforce data, generating actionable insights, and proactively identifying talent risks.

---

## Project Goal

Develop an end-to-end People Analytics platform capable of:

- Centralizing HR information
- Creating a single source of truth
- Monitoring workforce KPIs
- Identifying attrition risks
- Supporting executive decision-making
- Providing AI-generated workforce recommendations

---

## Scope

### Included

- Workforce analytics
- Attrition analysis
- Attendance analysis
- Performance analysis
- Tableau dashboards
- GenAI Workforce Advisor

### Excluded

- Payroll processing
- Employee self-service applications
- HR transaction systems

---

## Key Performance Indicators

### Workforce

- Headcount
- Active Employees

### Retention

- Attrition Rate
- Attrition Risk Score

### Attendance

- Absenteeism Rate
- Overtime Hours

### Performance

- Performance Score
- Workforce Health Score

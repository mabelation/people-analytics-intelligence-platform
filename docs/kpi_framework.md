# KPI Framework

## Executive Workforce KPIs

### Headcount
Total active employees.

### Attrition Rate
Percentage of employees leaving the company.

### Retention Rate
Percentage of employees retained.

### Workforce Health Score
Composite score based on performance, attendance and wellbeing indicators.

### Attrition Risk Score

Based on:
- Satisfaction

### High-Risk Employees
